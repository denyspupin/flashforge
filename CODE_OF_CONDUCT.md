# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming place regardless of background, identity, or experience level. Everyone who interacts with the project — in issues, pull requests, comments, or anywhere else — is expected to follow this code.

## Expected behaviour

- Be welcoming. Assume good faith.
- Be respectful. Disagreement is fine; personal attacks are not.
- Be constructive. Critique the work, not the person.
- Listen. If someone tells you that something you said or did was unwelcome, hear them out.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language of any kind.
- Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation.
- Publishing another person's private information without consent.
- Disruptive behaviour in issues, pull requests, or community spaces.

## Enforcement

The owner is responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct. Instances of unacceptable behaviour can be reported privately — see [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) for the contact channel. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated, and the outcome will be proportionate to the behaviour.
